# Quality Criteria: Ophthalmology Instagram Carousel

## Scoring System

Each criterion is scored 1-10. Overall score is the average.
- **APPROVE**: Overall >= 7/10 AND no criterion below 4/10
- **REJECT**: Overall < 7/10 OR any criterion below 4/10
- **CONDITIONAL APPROVE**: Overall >= 7/10 with minor issues (4-6 range on non-critical criteria)

---

## Content Quality Criteria

### 1. Medical Accuracy (Critical — hard reject below 4)
- [ ] Every medical claim is supported by a verifiable source
- [ ] No outdated or debunked medical information
- [ ] Correct terminology used (even when simplified for patients)
- [ ] Statistics cited with source and date
- [ ] No oversimplification that leads to misinformation

### 2. CFM Compliance (Critical — hard reject below 4)
- [ ] No language guaranteeing results ("100% de sucesso", "garantia de cura")
- [ ] No superlatives ("melhor", "mais avançado", "único")
- [ ] No patient identification in images (even with consent)
- [ ] Before/after content shows range of outcomes (4+ patients, satisfactory + unsatisfactory)
- [ ] No giveaways (sorteios) or influencer partnerships
- [ ] Profile displays CRM/AM number and RQE
- [ ] Educational framing for all clinical content

### 3. Hook Effectiveness (Score 1-10)
- [ ] First slide stops the scroll in under 3 seconds
- [ ] Hook uses a proven pattern (curiosity gap, bold claim, question, fear, numbered list)
- [ ] First 125 characters of caption create urgency to tap "...mais"
- [ ] Hook is specific to ophthalmology (not generic wellness)

### 4. Slide Content Quality (Score 1-10)
- [ ] Each slide has 40-80 words (headline + supporting text)
- [ ] Two-layer text hierarchy: bold headline + smaller supporting text
- [ ] Each slide advances the narrative (no filler or repetition)
- [ ] Every slide gives a reason to swipe to the next
- [ ] Medical jargon translated to patient-friendly Portuguese (grade 7-8)

### 5. CTA & Engagement Design (Score 1-10)
- [ ] Last slide has a specific, actionable CTA
- [ ] CTA matches content type (save for reference, share to someone, comment, agendar)
- [ ] Caption ends with an open-ended question or direct call
- [ ] Content designed for saves OR shares (not just likes)
- [ ] Mid-carousel soft CTA present ("salve para consultar depois")

### 6. Caption Quality (Score 1-10)
- [ ] Hook within first 125 characters
- [ ] Line breaks for mobile readability (max 3 lines per paragraph)
- [ ] SEO keywords present naturally ("oftalmologista em Manaus", "saúde ocular")
- [ ] 5 hashtags, mix of niche and location-based
- [ ] No links in caption (Instagram doesn't support clickable links)

### 7. Visual Design Quality (Score 1-10)
- [ ] Consistent design system across all slides (colors, fonts, spacing)
- [ ] All text meets minimum font sizes (Hero 58px, Heading 43px, Body 34px)
- [ ] WCAG AA contrast ratio 4.5:1 for all text
- [ ] Background colors alternate for visual rhythm
- [ ] Key phrases highlighted in accent color
- [ ] No slide number counters
- [ ] Self-contained HTML (no external dependencies except Google Fonts)
- [ ] Renders correctly at 1080x1440px

### 8. Brand Alignment (Score 1-10)
- [ ] CEDOA/Dr. Russo visual identity present on cover slide
- [ ] Tone: educativo, empático, autoritativo, confiável
- [ ] Language: accessible Portuguese, no elitism
- [ ] Regional pride elements when appropriate (Manaus, Amazônia)
- [ ] Consistent with company.md persona guidelines

---

## Veto Conditions (Automatic Reject)

1. Any medical claim without a verifiable source
2. CFM violation (guaranteed results, superlatives, patient identification)
3. Font size below platform minimum (20px absolute floor)
4. Missing CTA on final slide
5. Caption exceeds 2,200 characters
6. Fewer than 40 words on any content slide
7. Hook slide fails scroll-stop test (generic, vague, or unclear)
